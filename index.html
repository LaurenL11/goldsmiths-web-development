New Rules at Goldsmiths Land

  • No gum chewing. Last year we spent an estimated $430M cleaning gum off mascot uniforms.
  • Parents may not visit the park without child supervision.
  • No frowns. People frowning will be asked to leave.
  • We ask that you please not feed the Basilisks.
  • Keep hands, arms, legs and feet inside the ride at all times.
  • Do not board a ride without trousers.
  • Remain seated in the ride until it comes to a complete stop and you are instructed to do the hokey pokey.
  • Obey listed age, height, weight, and health restrictions.
  • Do not board a ride pissed out of your skull.
  • Never force anyone, especially children, to ride attractions they don't want to ride.
  • No Karens.
